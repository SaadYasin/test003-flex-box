Who We Are

Flora is the one-stop-shop to punctuate all life's moments. We believe everyday has something worth celebrating and flowers, plants and gifts make it so much brighter

Since sending our first birthday blooms in 1998, Floral now delivers florist-crafted arrangements, drop-ship bundles, fresh plants, and partners with trending gifting brands to ensure every one get the gift they'll love.
